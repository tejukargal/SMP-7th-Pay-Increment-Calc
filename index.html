Range: ₹56,100 – ₹1,82,400  |  40 cells  |  ~3% annual increment

Level 13A.1 – HOD / Principal (AGP 9000)

Range: ₹1,31,400 – ₹2,04,700  |  16 cells  |  ~3% annual increment

Designation Mapping: Lecturer → Level 9A  |  HOD / Principal → Level 13A.1

Pay Fixation: Old Basic × 2.57 (fitment factor), then fix at next cell in respective level.

Increment Rule: Annual increment = one cell progression in the pay matrix (~3% of basic).

Increment Date: 1 January or 1 July depending on date of joining / promotion.
